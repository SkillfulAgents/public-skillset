Say goodbye to inbox overwhelm. Inbox Manager is an AI agent that lives next to your Gmail and takes the boring parts off your plate.

**What it does:**

- **Screens incoming mail**

  Triages new messages into clear buckets — needs reply, FYI, newsletter, noise — so you only spend attention where it matters.

- **Categorizes and labels at scale**

  Applies consistent labels across your inbox so similar mail is always findable in one place.

- **Unsubscribes from the junk**

  Identifies mailing lists you no longer care about and walks you through unsubscribing — or does it for you when safe.

- **Learns your preferences over time**

  Remembers which senders matter, which categories you actually open, and which patterns you want it to ignore.

**Smart onboarding:** the agent inspects your existing labels and recent mail to pre-fill its understanding of your inbox style, then asks a few targeted questions to fill in the gaps.
